## ğŸ“‹ RAID ASSIGNMENTS

### **Tank Assignments**

#### **Phase 1: Darkness**
- **Tank 1:** Lu'lin (face away from raid)
- **Tank 2:** Beast of Nightmares when spawned
  - Kill Beast solo (only you can damage)
  - Minimize healing (reduce Corrupted Healing stacks)
  - Use self-healing/defensives
- **Swap:** When Tank 2's Beast dies, swap Lu'lin

#### **Phase 2: Daylight**
- **Both Tanks:** Alternate tanking Suen
- **Swap at:** 2-3 stacks Fan of Flames
- **Active Tank:** Position Suen next to Ice Comet
  - Stand IN Icy Shadows buff
  - Watch for Flames of Passion charge
- **Off-Tank:** Prepare to taunt, stay spread

#### **Phase 3: Break of Dusk**
- **Tank 1:** Suen (near Ice Comets)
- **Tank 2:** Lu'lin (away from raid)
- Keep bosses separated
- Suen priority for comet positioning

#### **Phase 4: Final**
- Single tank on remaining boss
- Other tank can DPS or help with mechanics

### **DPS Assignments**

#### **Phase 1: Darkness (0:00-3:00)**

**Primary Target:** Lu'lin (main DPS)

**When Suen Appears (Tears of the Sun):**
- **ALL DPS:** Switch to Suen
- Apply DoTs/bleeds immediately
- Burst damage during entire channel
- **Goal:** Suen below 90% HP by 3:00

**Beast of Nightmares:**
- Only fixated tank damages
- Everyone else ignores

**Lurkers in the Night (Heroic):**
- Ignore during phase (invisible)
- Kill at 3:00 when they appear (or during first Nuclear Inferno)

**Positioning:**
- Spread for Cosmic Barrage
- Avoid Moon Lotus clouds
- Ranged stay far from melee (Cosmic Barrage targets ranged only)

#### **Phase 2: Daylight (3:00-6:00)**

**Primary Target:** Suen (exclusive DPS)

**Key Mechanics:**
- Dodge Ice Comet projectiles (move from target location)
- Let Ice Comets spawn, don't kill them
- Suen must stay near comets (tank responsibility)
- **Use Heroism/Bloodlust here**
- **Use Chi-Ji (Rush of Cranes) here**

**During Nuclear Inferno:**
- Stack near Ice Comet (Icy Shadows = 60% Fire reduction)
- Cannot DPS Suen (immune)
- Good time to kill leftover Lurkers from P1

**Goal:** Suen below 50% HP by 6:00

#### **Phase 3: Break of Dusk (6:00-Kill)**

**Primary Target:** Suen (burn to 0%)

**Secondary Target:** Lu'lin (only during Nuclear Inferno)

**Tidal Force:**
- Dodge water waves (very fast, very deadly)
- Use Tiger's Celerity to slow them
- Healing/defensive cooldowns
- Lu'lin immune during this cast

**Positioning:**
- Spread for Light of Day
- Stack near Ice Comet during Nuclear Inferno
- Avoid all fire trails from previous phases

**Goal:** Kill Suen before 8:00

#### **Phase 4: Final Phase**

**Final Darkness (Suen dead):**
- DPS Lu'lin to 0%
- Avoid mechanics from P1
- Easy mode

**Final Daylight (Lu'lin dead):**
- DPS Suen to 0%
- **ALL COOLDOWNS**
- Blazing Radiance stacks infinitely
- Extreme damage race

### **Healer Assignments**

**Phase-Specific Priorities:**

**Phase 1: Darkness**
- Moderate raid damage (Tears of the Sun)
- Beast tank = light healing (minimize Corrupted Healing)
- Cosmic Barrage = spot heals
- Let healers' Corrupted Healing stacks fall off naturally

**Phase 2: Daylight**
- Heavy sustained raid damage
- Fan of Flames tank = high priority
- Nuclear Inferno = EXTREME (stack near comet)
- Plan cooldown rotation for Inferno casts

**Phase 3: Break of Dusk**
- HIGHEST healing phase
- Tidal Force = raid-wide burst
- Nuclear Inferno + Tidal Force = deadly combo
- Cooldowns on strict rotation

**Phase 4**
- Final Darkness: Low healing
- Final Daylight: Increasing damage (Blazing Radiance stacks)

**Cooldown Rotation (Break of Dusk):**
