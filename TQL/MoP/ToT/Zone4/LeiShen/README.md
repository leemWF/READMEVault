## ðŸ“‹ PHASE ONE STRATEGY (100-65%)

### Positioning & Movement
1. Keep boss near conduits in rotation - move every 10-20 sec
2. **Never let conduit reach 100 Energy** before transition
3. **Target conduit to overload:** East (Diffusion Chain) - leave it at 86-90 Energy
   - This conduit gets destroyed after Transition 1

### Conduit Rotation
- Start **East** (target) â†’ keep until 86-90 Energy
- Move **clockwise/counter-clockwise** to adjacent conduit â†’ 60-70 Energy
- Continue rotation through all 4 conduits
- Minimizes time away from conduits = less Discharged Energy

### Tank Assignments
- **Decapitate timing:** Off-tank taunts â†’ main tank runs 20-30y away FAST
- Use mobility cooldowns (Heroic Leap, Roll, etc.)
- Debuff prevents tanking for 30s (handled by rotation)

### Raid Positioning
- Stay mobile for Thunderstruck
- Avoid Crashing Thunder void zones
- Handle conduit mechanics as described above

## âš¡ FIRST TRANSITION (45 seconds)

**All 4 conduit abilities active simultaneously**

### Group Assignments
Split raid into **4 groups** covering 4 quarters:
- **10-man:** 2 groups of 2, 2 groups of 3
- **25-man:** 3 groups of 6, 1 group of 7

**Critical:** Groups must soak all Bouncing Bolts in their quarter (prevent add spawns)

### Execution
- Each group handles all 4 conduit mechanics in their area
- Stack for Static Shock, spread for Diffusion Chain
- Move to Overcharged players, soak Bouncing Bolts
- Healers position on borders between quarters (10m)
