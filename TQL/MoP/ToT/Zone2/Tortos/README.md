# Tortos - Raid Leader Strategy Guide

## üìã **Fight Overview**
Tortos is a **stationary boss encounter** with a unique turtle-kicking mechanic. The fight revolves around killing Whirl Turtle adds, kicking them at the boss to interrupt his raid-wiping breath attack, and managing waves of Vampiric Cave Bats. The fight is mechanically simple but requires coordination and execution.

**Core Mechanic:** Kill Whirl Turtles ‚Üí Kick them at boss to interrupt Furious Stone Breath ‚Üí Repeat

**Soft Enrage:** ~8 minutes - massive wave of Whirl Turtles overwhelms raid

---

## üê¢ **THE UNIQUE MECHANIC: TURTLE KICKING**

### **How Turtle Kicking Works:**
1. Whirl Turtles spawn and move randomly around room
2. When near death, turtle casts **Shell Block** - becomes immune and stationary
3. All raid members get **Kick Shell** extra action button when near a Shell Blocked turtle
4. Face the direction you want to kick, then press extra action button
5. Turtle flies through air toward target
6. If turtle hits enemy: **Interrupts spell + 25% damage taken debuff for 20 seconds**

**This is essential for interrupting Tortos' Furious Stone Breath!**

## üî¥ **HEROIC MODE CHANGES**

### **‚ö° The Two Critical Changes**

**1. Quake Stomp = 100% Max HP Damage**
- Up from 65% in Normal mode
- **Will instantly kill anyone not properly shielded**
- This is the main challenge of Heroic mode

**2. Humming Crystals** (NEW MECHANIC)

**How Humming Crystals Work:**
- Several crystals scattered around room
- Attack crystal with direct damage spell ‚Üí Gain shield absorbing 15% max HP
- **Heals increase shield size** (up to max 75% of max HP)
- Heals do NOT heal the player, only increase shield capacity
- No internal cooldown - can refresh constantly
- Entire raid can have shields simultaneously

**Critical Shield Mechanics:**
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Once shielded, player becomes UNHEALABLE until shield breaks**
- Low-HP players should get healed BEFORE taking shield
- Shields break from: Quake Stomp damage, Rockfall damage, Snapping Bite (tanks)
- Crystals take damage but have massive HP (won't die accidentally)

## üí° **CRITICAL TIPS**

### **General Tips**

‚úÖ **Boss Position:** Tortos is STATIONARY - never moves
‚úÖ **Tank in Melee Range:** CRITICAL - Tank must NEVER leave melee range or Growing Fury accelerates Furious Stone Breath
‚úÖ **Turtle Timing:** 3 Whirl Turtles spawn every 60s, Furious Stone Breath every 45s = Always have turtles available
‚úÖ **First Breath Critical:** First Whirl Turtles spawn shortly before first Furious Stone Breath - ALL DPS help kill first turtle fast
‚úÖ **Shell Concussion Window:** After kicking turtle, Tortos takes 25% increased damage for 20 seconds - Use offensive cooldowns
‚úÖ **Rockfall Positioning:** Blue circles = stalactite warning - Move immediately
‚úÖ **Quake Stomp + Rockfall Combo:** Stomp increases Rockfall frequency for 8 seconds - Extra vigilant during this time
‚úÖ **DPS Priority Discipline:** Ranged on Whirl Turtles, Melee on Tortos/Bats - Don't deviate

### **Tank Tips**

‚úÖ **Tortos Tank (Tank 1):**
- NEVER leave melee range of Tortos
- Boss mods warn of Snapping Bite - Use active mitigation for each
- Be topped off before each Snapping Bite (~60% HP damage)
- Snapping Bite has visible cast time - predictable
- **Heroic:** Always maintain shield from Humming Crystal

‚úÖ **Bat Tank (Tank 2):**
- Pick up bats as they spawn
- Move them next to Tortos for melee cleave
- **NEVER drop below 350k HP** - Allows bats to heal via Drain the Weak
- Bats are not hard hitting but will stack up if not killed
- **Heroic:** Always maintain shield from Humming Crystal

‚úÖ **Tank Swaps:** None required - roles are fixed

### **Healer Tips**

‚úÖ **Tank Healing Priority:**
- Tortos tank before each Snapping Bite
- Bat tank must stay above 350k HP always
- **Heroic:** Keep tank shields at max capacity (75% max HP)

‚úÖ **Raid Healing Priority:**
- Heavy damage after Quake Stomp (65% max HP Normal / 100% Heroic)
- Moderate damage from Rockfall hits
- Random damage from Rockfall + Whirl Turtle contact

‚úÖ **Heroic Shielding Strategy:**
- Heal shielded players BEFORE Quake Stomp to maximize shield size
- **Goal: Everyone at 75% shield capacity before each Stomp**
- Don't heal low-HP players who have shields (they're unhealable)
- Wait for shields to break before healing low-HP players

### **DPS Tips**

‚úÖ **Ranged DPS Priority:**
1. Whirl Turtles (always)
2. Tortos (when no turtles alive)
3. Help on first turtle (everyone needs to help kill it fast)

‚úÖ **Melee DPS Priority:**
1. Tortos (primary target with cleave on bats)
2. Vampiric Cave Bats (via cleave)
3. First Whirl Turtle (help ranged kill it fast)

‚úÖ **Interrupt Assignments:** One ranged DPS + one backup assigned to kick turtles at Furious Stone Breath
‚úÖ **Offensive Cooldowns:** Use during Shell Concussion debuff (25% increased damage taken)
‚úÖ **Heroic Shielding:** Maintain shield at all times, refresh frequently

### **Turtle Kicking Tips**

‚úÖ **Positioning for Kick:**
- Stand so turtle is between you and Tortos
- Face boss while standing behind turtle
- Use Kick Shell extra action button when Furious Stone Breath cast begins

‚úÖ **Timing:**
- Move into position 5-10 seconds before Furious Stone Breath
- Wait for cast to begin, then kick immediately
- Backup kicker ready at different turtle

‚úÖ **Common Mistakes:**
- Not facing the right direction
- Standing too far from turtle (extra action button won't appear)
- Getting knocked back by active Whirl Turtle
- Kicking too early or too late

‚úÖ **Safety:**
- Assign backup kicker in case primary gets knocked or makes mistake
- Multiple turtles should be Shell Blocked before each breath for options

## ‚ö° **HEROIC MODE STRATEGY BREAKDOWN**

### **The Shield Game**

**Phase 1: Shield Acquisition (Everyone, constantly)**
1. Attack Humming Crystal with direct damage spell
2. Receive 15% max HP shield
3. Get healed to 75% shield capacity
4. Refresh shield after it breaks

**Phase 2: Pre-Quake Stomp (Every ~30-40s)**
1. All players ensure shield active
2. Healers spam heals on shielded players
3. Goal: Everyone at 75% shield capacity
4. Quake Stomp hits for 100% max HP but shields absorb it

**Phase 3: Post-Quake Stomp**
1. Shields break from Stomp damage
2. Players now healable again
3. Top everyone off
4. Repeat from Phase 1

### **Shield Priority List**
1. **Tanks** - Always maintain 75% shields (they take constant damage)
2. **Low-HP players** - Heal first, THEN shield (or they stay low HP)
3. **Everyone else** - Maintain shields, refresh after Quake Stomp

### **Common Heroic Mistakes**
‚ùå Shielding low-HP players (they become unhealable at low HP)
‚ùå Not healing shielded players before Quake Stomp (shields too small)
‚ùå Forgetting to refresh shields after they break
‚ùå Tanks dropping shields (they die to Snapping Bite)

### **Alternative Strategy: Bat Kiting (If DPS Tight)**
- Some guilds kite Vampiric Cave Bats instead of killing
- Monk tank ideal for mobility
- Regular stuns on bats
- Not recommended if you have DPS for kill

## üìù **PULL SEQUENCE**

### **Normal Mode:**

1. **Pre-pull:**
   - Assign primary + backup turtle kickers
   - Confirm DPS priority (ranged on turtles, melee on boss/bats)
   - Position raid spread for Rockfall avoidance

2. **Pull (0:00):**
   - Tank 1 engages Tortos, stays in melee range
   - Tank 2 ready for first bat wave

3. **First Turtles (0:15):**
   - ALL DPS burn first turtle to Shell Block
   - Must have one ready before first Furious Stone Breath

4. **First Bats (0:30):**
   - Tank 2 picks up, brings to Tortos
   - Melee cleave while on Tortos

5. **First Breath (0:45):**
   - Primary kicker kicks turtle at Tortos
   - Backup ready in case of failure
   - Boss gains Shell Concussion

6. **Bloodlust Window (0:45-1:05):**
   - Pop Bloodlust + all DPS cooldowns
   - Tortos takes 25% extra damage

7. **Repeat (1:00 onwards):**
   - Maintain DPS priorities
   - Avoid Rockfall
   - Heal through Quake Stomp
   - Interrupt every Furious Stone Breath

8. **Kill before 8:00 soft enrage**

### **Heroic Mode:**

1. **Pre-pull (Everything from Normal Mode, plus):**
   - Explain Humming Crystal shield mechanic
   - Emphasize: Low-HP players heal BEFORE shielding
   - Assign healers to focus shield maximizing

2. **Pull (0:00):**
   - Everyone immediately gets shield from crystal
   - Healers begin maximizing shields to 75%

3. **Ongoing Shield Maintenance:**
   - Refresh shields after each Quake Stomp
   - Healers spam shielded players before each Stomp
   - Goal: Everyone at 75% shield for every Stomp

4. **Low-HP Shield Protocol:**
   - Player at low HP: Call out
   - Healers: Top them off FIRST
   - Then player gets shield
   - Never shield while low HP (can't be healed after)

5. **Everything else same as Normal Mode**
