## ğŸ“‹ RAID ASSIGNMENTS

### **Tank Assignments**

**Tank 1 (Primordius Tank):**
- Face boss away from raid
- Taunt at 3-4 stacks of Malformed Blood
- Move boss away from Volatile Pools
- Kill Living Fluids when off-tanking

**Tank 2 (Viscous Horror Tank):**
- Tank Viscous Horrors 10+ yards from boss
- Taunt at 3-4 stacks of Black Blood
- Never let add reach Primordius
- Position adds for cleave when possible

**Tank Swap Rotation:**
- Swap every 3-4 stacks of respective debuffs
- Coordinate taunts over voice
- Off-tank helps with Living Fluid cleanup

### **DPS Assignments**

**Phase 1 - Not Fully Mutated (0-4 stacks):**
- **Priority:** Kill Living Fluids ONLY
- Collect Mutagenic Pools to gain mutation stacks
- Never kill fluids near boss (Volatile Pool risk)
- Highest DPS players collect stacks first

**Phase 2 - Fully Mutated (5 stacks, 2 min buff):**
- **Priority:** DPS Primordius exclusively
- **CRITICAL:** Avoid ALL Mutagenic Pools (harmful debuffs)
- Help on Viscous Horror if needed
- Use cooldowns during Heroism window

**Phase 3 - Reset Cycle:**
- Return to killing Living Fluids
- Collect 5 new mutation stacks
- Stagger transformations (melee first, then ranged)

**Final Phase (Last 90s):**
- All players DPS boss exclusively
- Ignore remaining adds
- Use all cooldowns

### **Healer Assignments**

**Spread healing across:**
- Tank damage from both bosses
- Raid-wide AoE mechanics
- Random target debuffs

**Cooldown rotation based on active mutations:**
- Ventral Sacs = Heavy raid AoE, plan cooldowns
