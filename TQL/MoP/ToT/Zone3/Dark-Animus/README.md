## ğŸ¯ RAID ASSIGNMENTS

### **Tank Assignments**

**Tank 1 (Dark Animus):**
- Face boss away from raid
- Watch for Anima Ring - exit immediately
- Taunt when other tank gets Anima Ring debuff
- Move out of Explosive Slam indicators
- Prepare for Matter Swap position swaps

**Tank 2 (Massive Golems):**
- Pick up Massive Golems as they activate
- Tank in CENTER of room (prevents Anima Golem chaos during Matter Swap)
- Face away from raid
- Watch for Explosive Slam - dodge ground indicators
- Coordinate dispel timing for Matter Swap
- Late fight: Rotate with DPS to handle Empower Golem stacks

**Off-Tank Responsibilities:**
- When not tanking boss, help with Anima Golem collection
- When boss tank gets Anima Ring debuff, taunt immediately
- Can pick up Massive Golem temporarily during Matter Swap chaos

### **DPS Assignments**

**Phase 1 - Anima Golem Execution:**

**Designated Golem Killers (2-3 DPS):**
- Assigned to kill Anima Golems in specific locations
- **CRITICAL:** Must position correctly before killing
- Use marks/waypoints for Massive Golem positions
- Stop all AoE/DoTs when golem is near death

**Boss DPS (Remaining DPS):**
- Focus Dark Animus from pull
- Use cooldowns early (Heroism at pull in 25M)
- Switch to help finish Anima Golems if falling behind schedule
- **After 2:00:** Exclusive boss DPS

**Anima Golem Tanking:**
- Plate DPS and tanky specs help tank Anima Golems
- **NEVER** let golems get within melee range of each other
- Keep 10+ yard spacing at all times
- Stop damaging golem once assigned killer takes over

### **Healer Assignments**

**Healer Priority:**
1. Tanks (especially during Empower Golem stacks)
2. Matter Swap targets (huge burst healing needed)
3. Touch of Animus DoT targets
4. Anima Font affected players (if they fail positioning)
5. Interrupting Jolt raid damage

**Cooldown Rotation (Post-2:00):**
- Every 30s: Rotate healing cooldowns
- At 76 Anima: Major cooldown for first Interrupting Jolt
- Save externals for Matter Swap on low health tanks
- Conserve mana - fight is long

**Dispel Assignment:**
- Assign 1-2 healers to watch Matter Swap
- Dispel after **4-5 seconds** (optimal damage split)
- Too early = tank takes full damage
- Too late = swapped player takes massive damage
- Both targets must be topped before dispel

## âš”ï¸ ABILITY HANDLING

### **Touch of Animus** 
- Random player gets permanent Fire DoT
- Stacks throughout fight
- Healing intensive later
- **Affected players:** Note your debuff count for healers

### **Anima Ring** (10+ Anima)
- Ring of spheres closes on tank
- Tank MUST exit (consumes 1 sphere)
- Applies: Fire DoT + 50% increased melee damage (15s)
- **Action:** Exit ring, taunt swap immediately

### **Anima Font** (25+ Anima)
- Touch of Animus targets now radiate AoE damage
- Affected players deal damage to nearby allies
- **Action:** Spread 8+ yards from all players

### **Interrupting Jolt** (75+ Anima)
- Raid-wide Nature damage
- 8.5s silence if caught casting
- **Action:** Stop ALL casts when boss begins casting

### **Matter Swap** (Massive Golems)
- Debuff on tank, lasts 12s
- Swaps position with farthest player
- Damage = tank's max HP, split between both
- **Action:** Dispel after 4-5s, top both targets first

### **Explosive Slam** (Massive Golems)
- Obvious ground indicator
- 12y AoE + stacking debuff
- **Action:** Move out of red circle

### **Empower Golem** (Heroic)
- Boss heals lowest HP golem by 3%
- Grants permanent +24% damage (stacks)
- Late fight: Massive Golems hit VERY hard
- **Action:** Tank swaps, externals, major cooldowns
